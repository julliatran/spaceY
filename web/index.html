About Us

PURPOSE: This project aims to optimize the use of spaces including conference rooms, study rooms that are reservable for users by removing “not showing” reservations. At present, rooms at CMU are difficult to reserve because most of the time all rooms are booked. On the contrary, many people just don’t show up for the reservation because there is no punishment which causes a huge portion of available rooms wasted that could help to build great ideas and constructive conversation.

  • Confirm the meeting took place with check-ins. If nobody shows up after a set amount of time, the room unbooks itself.
  • Report room issues.
  • Hear about issues before they become problems.
  • Get alerts when equipment breaks.
  • Color label system.
